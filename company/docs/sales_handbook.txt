SALES TEAM HANDBOOK

SALES TARGETS
Each sales representative is assigned a quarterly target of $50,000.
Team leads are expected to achieve $80,000 per quarter.
Annual targets are set in January and reviewed at mid-year.
Targets may be revised based on market conditions with 30 days notice.

COMMISSION STRUCTURE
Sales reps earn 5% commission on all deals closed within their quarterly target.
Any sales above the quarterly target earn 8% commission (accelerator).
Commission is paid monthly on the 15th of the following month.
Deals must be fully paid by the client before commission is released.

LEAD MANAGEMENT
All leads must be logged in the CRM within 24 hours of first contact.
Leads older than 60 days without activity are reassigned to other team members.
Each rep is responsible for following up with leads at least once every 7 days.
Lost deals must be marked with a reason code in the CRM for reporting purposes.

CLIENT ONBOARDING
New clients must complete the onboarding checklist within 14 days of signing.
Every new client is assigned a dedicated account manager for the first 90 days.
Client satisfaction surveys are sent at 30, 60, and 90 days post-onboarding.
Any client escalation must be reported to the sales manager within 2 hours.

TOOLS AND RESOURCES
The company uses Salesforce as the primary CRM tool.
Sales decks and product collateral are stored in the shared Google Drive folder.
Competitive intelligence reports are updated monthly by the marketing team.
Sales training sessions are held every first Monday of the month at 10 AM.
