COMPANY HR POLICY DOCUMENT

LEAVE POLICY
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year.
Sick leave is capped at 10 days per year with a medical certificate required after 3 consecutive days.
Maternity leave is 26 weeks fully paid. Paternity leave is 2 weeks fully paid.
Leave must be applied at least 5 working days in advance except for emergencies.

WORKING HOURS
Standard working hours are 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Friday.
Employees may opt for flexible working hours between 8 AM and 8 PM with manager approval.
Remote work is allowed up to 3 days per week for all departments.
Overtime is compensated at 1.5x the hourly rate for weekdays and 2x for weekends.

PERFORMANCE REVIEW
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year — in June and December.
Employees are rated on a scale of 1 to 5 across four categories: output quality, teamwork, communication, and initiative.
A rating of 4 or above qualifies an employee for a merit-based salary increment of up to 15%.
Employees rated below 2 for two consecutive reviews will be placed on a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP).

EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
Business travel expenses are reimbursed within 30 days of submission.
Meal allowance during travel is capped at $50 per day.
All expenses above $200 require prior manager approval.
Receipts must be submitted via the company expense portal within 15 days of incurring the expense.

CODE OF CONDUCT
Employees must maintain professional behavior in all interactions.
Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind will result in immediate disciplinary action.
Confidential company data must not be shared outside the organization without written approval.
Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to HR within 5 working days of arising.
